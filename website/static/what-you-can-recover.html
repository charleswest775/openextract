Honest note

What's not in a backup.

iPhone backups contain what was on the phone at the moment the backup ran. If a message was deleted before the backup, no tool — free or paid — can recover it from that backup, because it was never there.

A few things Apple deliberately never puts in backups: Face ID/Touch ID biometric data, Apple Pay card numbers, Keychain items from unencrypted backups, and anything from apps with "Don't back up this app" toggled in Settings. That's not an OpenExtract limitation — it's the format.
