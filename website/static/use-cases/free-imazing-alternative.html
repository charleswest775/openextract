Alternative

A free, open-source
alternative to iMazing.

If you just need to get data out of an iPhone backup — messages, photos, contacts, call history — OpenExtract does that part, for free, with source code you can read.

iMazing is a good tool. It's also paid software, closed source, and Windows-and-Mac only. If the only reason you're looking at it is to pull data out of an iPhone backup, you don't need the whole suite. You need an extractor.

That's what OpenExtract is. Nothing else — no music sync, no app management, no live device browsing. Just: point it at a backup, get the data out.

Why people switch
It's actually free.
No trial, no paywall, no "unlock to export more than 3 items." MIT license, installer you download once, works forever.
You can read the code.
Every line of OpenExtract is on GitHub. If your workplace or your conscience requires auditable tooling, this is the shape that takes.
Linux.
iMazing is Windows and Mac. OpenExtract runs on Linux too — useful for forensics workstations and Linux users who just want their data.
No account.
Nothing to register for. Nothing to cancel. Nothing phones home.
Your existing iMazing backups work.
iMazing writes standard Apple backups. OpenExtract reads standard Apple backups. You don't need to re-back-up anything.

Where iMazing still wins

Being honest: if you need more than extraction, iMazing still has advantages.

  • Live device management. Transferring apps, music, ringtones between phones — OpenExtract doesn't do that.
  • Paid support on a deadline. When something breaks at 5pm before a filing, a vendor with a support team is different from GitHub issues.
  • iMazing Forensics Edition. Turnkey chain-of-custody and hash verification workflow for expert-witness use.
  • Maturity. Over a decade of edge cases across iOS versions.

See the full comparison for the complete feature matrix.

Switching is easy
Your backups stay where they are.

Install OpenExtract, point it at your existing backup folder, done. You don't need to re-back-up your iPhone or uninstall iMazing.
